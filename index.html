Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ball Sort Ultimate Color Puzzle. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

We do not directly collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII) (such as your name, email address, or phone number). However, the app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device.

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads and analyze performance. This data may include:

  • Device Identifiers: such as the Advertising ID (GAID) or IDFA.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the ads (clicks, impressions).
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from IP addresses to serve relevant ads.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to ensure the ads run smoothly.

Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:

The information collected by third-party services is used for:

We and our partners (like AdMob) use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like Mobile Advertising IDs) to analyze trends and track user behavior within the app to improve the ad experience.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at appstudioultimate@gmail.com so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

9. Data Deletion Request & Process

We provide two ways for users to delete their data, ensuring compliance with Google Play's Data Safety policies:

A. In-App Deletion (Instant)

Users can reset their game progress and clear all locally stored data directly within the app settings. This action immediately purges:

  • Level progress (stored in LocalStorage)
  • Game preferences and settings
  • Cached web data and cookies

B. Manual Deletion Request

If you wish for us to delete any other associated identifiers or if you have questions regarding your data, please contact us:

  • Email: appstudioultimate@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request

We will respond and confirm the deletion of any stored identifiers within 30 days of receiving your request.
